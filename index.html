Voice Actor

Liebe has messy, white hair and is short. Most of his body was coated in black; a black line runs from his chest to under his chin, while two lines cross his red eyes. He had two bat-like wings, two ram-like horns, a long thin tail, pointed teeth, and clawed fingers and toes.

Liebe wanted a physical body connected to a five-leaf clover grimoire. His presence is also intimidating, as individuals, including Asta himself, tremble whenever they sense Liebe. He has a unique way of laughing, Ge-ge-ge 「ゲゲゲ, Viz: "Keh-keh-keh/Geh-heh-heh"」.

5-Leaf Clover Grimoire Black Clover

Anime

Black Clover

Type

Grimoire

Skill

Anti Magic

Owner

Asta

When the owner of the four-leaf clover grimoire falls into absolute despair, the book is corrupted by its owner's deep despair and hatred, becoming what is known as the Grimoire of Despair 「絶望の魔導書 Zetsubō no Gurimowāru」. During this process, the clover turns black and develops a fifth leaf.

This five-leaf clover grimoire can be picked up and possessed by demons, who use the books to manifest their true power. Unlike ordinary grimoires, the five-leaf clover grimoire is not destroyed after the death of its owner. Instead, it will be inactive until a new compatible user is born and comes of age.
